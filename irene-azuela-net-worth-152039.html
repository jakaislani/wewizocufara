About

Mexican Actress known for her roles on El Hotel de los Secretos, La Querida del Centauro, and Amores Querer con Alevosia. She's also appeared in the films Miss Bala and Quemar las Naves.

She studied at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Arts and the Desmond Jones School. She made her first TV appearance in 2000.
