Best Cookies: Best Chocolate Chip Cookies and More

The best cookies at the grocery store are sweet, tasty, and satisfy a sweet tooth. Seriously, why bake when you can just buy cookies that are as good as homemade—and others that are so clearly manufactured in a factory that they have a charm all their own. From the best chocolate chip cookies to the best coconut macaroons, these are the best store bought cookies we’ve found in our taste tests.

Best Chocolate Chip

Tate’s Bake Shop Chocolate Chip CookiesTarget

If you love soft-baked cookies, we’re sorry to inform you that crispy chocolate chip cookies are where it’s at. In fact, the best chocolate chip cookies we found in our taste test were these thin, crispy cookies from Tate’s. They’re crispy, yes, but they are not dry by any stretch of the imagination. They’re downright buttery. They’re also loaded with chocolate chips. This is one of the best cookies for chocolate lovers, for sure.

Best Peanut Butter

Back to Nature Peanut Butter Creme CookiesInstacart

Like most peanut butter lovers, we’re nuts about Nutter Butter, but these suspiciously healthy looking sandwich cookies are actually even more delicious. Back to Nature Peanut Butter Creme Cookies have that perfect sweet-salty balance, and manage to be crunchy while still being nice and tender. Big bonus for plant-based peeps: They’re vegan!

Best Oreo

Extra Stuf Oreo ThinsWalmart

If you consider Oreos to be among the best cookies at the grocery store, but you’ve always had this nagging feeling that the cookie-to-creme ratio is a little bit off, Extra Stuf Oreo Thins are a godsend. As Sporked senior writer Jordan Myrick put it, these Oreos have “the exact amount of creme and cookie you want.” The cookies are thin and snappy and there’s just the right amount of creme. We tasted a lot of Oreos and these are the best Oreos of them all.

Best Girl Scout

Caramel deLites/SamoasGirl Scouts

When Girl Scout cookie season rolls around, we sincerely hope you’re stocking up on Samoas (aka Caramel deLites, depending on where you live and which GS bakery your cookies come from). These aren’t just the best Girl Scout cookies; they’re some of the best cookies, period. They’re crunchy but chewy, and absolutely covered in toasty, tasty coconut shreds. Yes, Thin Mints are great, but these are just so special.

Best Animal Crackers

Kroger the Wild Side Animal CookiesInstacart

Yes, these are called “crackers,” but we all count them as cookies, right? They’re the best cookies when you want something that’s just a touch sweet but still plenty satisfying. And these Kroger animal crackers are basically perfect—they’re a little citrusy, a little vanilla-y, and have just the right amount of crunch. Some animal crackers are just too crackery, but these are definitely closer to the cookie end of the spectrum.
