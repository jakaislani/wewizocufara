Joshua Buatsi Trainer

Joshua Buatsi trainer is a well-known boxing coach, Virgil Hunter.

The American native boxing instructor has worked with a number of world champions like Andre Ward, Amir Khan, and Andre Berto.

He is well-regarded in the boxing world for his ability to train fighters and help them realize their maximum potential.

Moreover, the coach is noted for emphasizing defense and technique. 2018 saw the start of Buatsi’s training relationship with Hunter, and since then, Buatsi has won every bout he has fought.

Joshua’s ascent to the status of the top light-heavyweight boxer was significantly influenced by Hunter, who is renowned for placing a major emphasis on the physical and mental development of boxers.

Joshua Buatsi Family

Joshua was born in Accra, Ghana, on March 14, 1993, but grew up in the UK.

In interviews, Buatsi claimed that his family emigrated to the UK when he was a little boy and that his parents encouraged him to pursue boxing.

Additionally, he has discussed the difficulties of growing up as an immigrant in the UK as well as the significance of his faith and Ghanaian ancestry in the formation of his identity.

His family settled in south London’s Thornton Heath after relocating to the UK.

The star went to Edenham High School to study and competed as an amateur boxer and earned a 2:1 degree in management with sports science from St. Mary’s University in Twickenham.

Moreover, Joshua is a reserved person who hasn’t revealed a lot of information about his family or personal life in the media.

Instead, he concentrated on his boxing career, where he is now a rising star.
