Top Facts about Mike Price

  • Mike Price was born on July 6, 1946.
  • He played college football at Everett Community College and Washington State University.
  • Price coached at Weber State, Washington State, UTEP, and Alabama.
  • His overall coaching record is 177-182.
  • In 1997, he led Washington State to the Rose Bowl.
  • Price was hired as Alabama’s head coach in 2003 but was fired before coaching a game due to off-field issues.
  • He returned to coaching at UTEP from 2004-2012.
  • Price retired from coaching in 2018 after serving as an offensive analyst for the University of Michigan.
  • He has been inducted into several halls of fame including the WSU Athletic Hall of Fame and the Pacific Northwest Football Hall of Fame.
  • As a player, he was a quarterback and punter at Everett CC and a receiver at WSU.
