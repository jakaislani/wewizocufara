Rande Gerber and Cindy Crawford Marital Relationship

Rande Gerber and his wife, Cindy Crawford are now regarded as one of the world's sweetest couples. Despite having a different career and a busy schedule, they are still enjoying their nuptials over two decades and have never had any disagreements or issues.

Well, Rande is a reputed businessman who is known as the founder of the nightlife industries named Midnight Oil and the Gerber Group. On the other hand, Cindy is a model, businesswoman and as well as model. Let's talk about their marital life today.

The Beautiful Wedding Ceremony

Gerber and his beautiful wife of 23 years Cindy has tied the wedding knot on 29 May 1998 in a private wedding ceremony. Their big day took place at the ocean club resort, Paradise Island's 58th room in the Bahamas.

The bride was wearing a white mini-piece shift dress, designed by Galliano while the groom donned a white simple plain shirt and dark black pant. At the time of their marriage, Rande was 36 while Cindy was four years younger than him ie. 32.

More than 90 guests were attending their traditional reception party including all of their family and friends. To make their wedding more memorable, the newlywed's duo enjoyed the beautiful dance together to the song by the band, Baha Men.

How Rande and Cindy First Met?

Gerber, the son of Jordan Gerber and Ellen Peckman, met his wife, Cindy in their 20s. After their first meeting, they became a close friend and got into a relationship.

Afterwards, the lovebirds took their romance to the next door and dedicated themselves to getting married in 1999. Ever since they maintain a healthy coupled life unitedly with full of respects for each other.  

Before tying the knot with Rande, the supermodel Crawford was once married to her first husband, Richard Gere, an American actor for over four years. They got hitched on December 12, 1991, at The Church of the West in Las Vegas.

Similarly, Angelina Jolie and Billy Bob Thornton were also married at the same church. However, they ended their marriage on 1st December 1995.

Parents Of Two Adult Children 

From their two decades of marital life, the husband and wife, are blessed with two grown-up kids, a daughter and a son. Now, both of them are pursuing their career in modelling. 

Their firstborn child, a son Presley Walker Gerber was born on 2nd July 1999. Their youngest child, a daughter, Kaia Jordan Gerber was born on 3rd September 2001. A report claims that their mating is on the rock due to their beautiful kids.
