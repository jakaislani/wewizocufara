Scott Cam – Biography

Scott Cam was born in Australia on November 25, 1962. The winner of both Silver and Gold Logie Awards for his hosting work on the Australian Nine Network reality series The Block, Cam is also well known for presenting the lifestyle programs Renovation Rescue and Domestic Blitz.

From 2000 to 2006, he made frequent appearances on the Jamie Durie-hosted program Backyard Blitz. After studying at Waverley College in Sydney, Australia, he became a carpenter’s apprentice and subsequently worked several odd jobs before establishing his own construction business.

Scott Cam’s Professional Career

Cam debuted on Nine’s famous lifestyle show Backyard Blitz in 2000, partnering with Jamie Durie, and did building parts until the show was canceled in 2006. He also hosted the 2010 season of The Block, which he has continued to host ever since.

In 2014, he received the Silver Logie Award for Most Popular Presenter and the Gold Logie Award for Most Popular Australian TV Personality for his work on The Block. Scott Cam is also a brand ambassador for Mitre 10 Australia, a position he has held since 2011.

Scott also serves as an Australian Government Apprenticeships Ambassador. When Cam responded to questioning regarding his ambassadorship with “none of your business,” he sparked outrage.
