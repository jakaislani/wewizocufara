Rich The Kid | Career

The young rapper started working at the age of 15 at Wendy’s Fast-food restaurant. Soon after realizing his love for music, he embarked upon the path to music. In 2010 he got his first studio session. He was only 16 at the time.

After his debut mixtape Been About The Benjamins in 2013, the rapper climbed the ranks of the Atlanta scene. His two singles New Freezer and Plug Walk, made it to the Billboard Hot 100.

In the year 2016, he started his own label, Rich Forever Music Label. To this date, he has signed seven artists to his record level, including himself.

This year he signed a multi-million dollar deal with Rostrum Records. He also inked deals with Interscope Records, 300 Entertainment, and Quality Control Music.

All these record labels have contributed to Rich The Kid’s net worth of $12 million.
