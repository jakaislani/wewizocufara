Real Reason I Did Butt Enlargement Surgery Mercy Eke

Big Brother Naija Season 4 winner, Mercy Eke, has opened up about her butt enlargement surgery and her motivation for it.

Following the unexpected death of prominent Port Harcourt influencer, Christabel, who died due to complications from a botched plastic surgery performed in Lagos, several celebrities offered their thoughts on the topic.

Mercy Eke, who had plastic surgery to improve her buttocks claims she had a good motive for going under the knife.

Mercy Eke said that she needed to have her body surgically enlarged in order to enhance her confidence, look nice in anything she wore, and sell her business.

The reality TV star and influencer, went on to say that body augmentation is the same as lash extensions and skin bleaching since they are both ways of boosting the body that God did not intend for them to be.

‘I did it for myself , I did it to enhance my confidence.. I wanted to look good in what I wear, I am into fashion…I did  for myself, I did it to sell my business, I don’t think there is anything to be ashamed of…”, she said.
