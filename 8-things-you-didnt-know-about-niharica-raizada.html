8 Things You Didn’t Know About Niharica Raizada

The actress has acted in many films for which she got much popularity but she became famous for appearing in the film named ‘Warrior Savitri’ in which she portrayed the role of Savitri. While it was released in the year 2016.

3- Highly-educated

The stunning actress, Niharica Raizada is a highly-educated lady who pursued the degree of Masters of Research in Translational Medicine and Research in Cardiology. Interesting to know that she is so talented and well-educated.

4- Hobbies

The actress loves dancing and singing in her leisure time as she considers them as one of the most enthralling and relaxing hobbies she loves to do.

5- Favorite Films

Among the most popular movies, some of the best are Bollywood movies including ‘Ishq Forever’ and ‘Kuch Kuch Hota Hai’. While her most favorite Hollywood movie is ‘Bag Boy Lover Boy’.

6- Trained Actress

The gorgeous actress, Niharica Raizada is a trained star who learned acting from the New York Film Academy and became a professionally trained actress. She then joined the field of showbiz and got many acting gigs.

7- Trained Dancer/Western Classical Music

Niharica Raizada is a well-trained dancer who got trained in Belly dance. Also, she got trained in the field of Western Classical Music and ballet. Sounds great? How much she is talented and blessed with multiple skills.

Once in an interview, the actress, Niharica Raizada revealed that she has a dream to work with a well-known Bollywood film director, Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The actress will soon get a chance to work with her dream director.
