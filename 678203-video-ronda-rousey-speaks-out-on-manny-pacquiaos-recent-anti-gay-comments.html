VIDEO: Ronda Rousey Speaks Out on Manny Pacquiaos Recent Anti-Gay Comments

TMZ cameras recently caught with UFC Superstar , and during the brief interview Rousey had the following to say about boxing champion Manny Pacquiao recently claiming homosexuals are “no better than animals”.

“I understand that a lot of people use religion as a reason to be against gay people, but, I mean, there was no ‘Thou shall not be gay,’ God never said that. I really think that our Pope now is boss. He was saying something the other day that religion should be all-encompassing and should be about welcoming everyone and loving everyone and not leaving people out. And I think sometimes people take the wrong message sometimes.”

Ronda Rousey
