CASI, Davenport, closes temporarily after flooding

The Center for Active Seniors Inc, Davenport, will be closed all next week through Friday, Jan. 26, because of a break in its fire suppression system, according to a news release.

ServPro is working to dry out floors, walls and ceiling spaces and next week will determine repairs and replacement that will need to be completed, the release says.

About 2:30 p.m. Wednesday, a water pipe connected to CASI’s fire suppression system burst and caused major flooding, according to a post on its Facebook page.

Updates will be provided on the CASI Facebook page or at the main office, 563-386-747.
